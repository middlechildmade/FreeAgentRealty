Free Agent Realty

Free Agent Realty was founded on the principal of protecting our client's equity as if it were our own. We set out to help property owners achieve the best return on investment in the industry. Our agents live and work in your community and provide the expert service you've come to expect from a traditional brokerage relationship.

The Free Agent Realty difference is that we strive for your loyalty rather than a commission check when selling your home. This important distinction results in substantial savings for our clients. Typically well into the thousands of dollars.

We understand that selling your home is often one of the most important transactions you will have in life and we have a passion for helping you maximize your results every step of the way.

About Me

I was born and raised in the Madison area and have deep multifaceted knowledge of the local real estate market. My diverse background includes experience in the sale and acquisition of residential and commercial property, real estate finance, foreclosure and short sales, renovation, real estate development, investment analysis, and construction management.

I take great pride in helping my clients achieve their goals weather it be finding the home of their dreams, selling their existing property for top dollar, or assisting them in meeting their investment objectives. Put my broad experience to work for you, I would be honored for the opportunity to earn your business.
